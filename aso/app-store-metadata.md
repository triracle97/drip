### Full Description (App Store / Google Play)

```
Stop paying for subscriptions you forgot about. Stop getting charged when free trials expire.

Drip is the subscription tracker that actually saves you money.

THE PROBLEM
The average person spends $219/month on subscriptions but thinks they spend $86. That's $1,596/year in hidden costs. Free trials silently convert to paid plans. Price increases slip by unnoticed.

HOW DRIP HELPS

-- Free Trial Tracking --
Add any free trial in seconds. Drip sends you alerts at 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day before it expires. Never get surprise-charged again.

-- Smart Reminders --
Set multiple reminders per subscription. Get notified 7 days before renewal, 1 day before, or on the day. Stack as many reminders as you need.

-- Subscription Dashboard --
See exactly what you spend monthly and yearly. Organized by category: streaming, music, fitness, productivity, and more.

-- Subscription Creep Alerts --
Drip detects when your total monthly spending increases and alerts you. Catch that extra subscription before it becomes invisible.

-- Cancel Guides --
One-tap access to cancellation pages for 500+ services. Netflix, Spotify, Adobe, gym memberships — cancel in seconds, not minutes.

-- Privacy First --
No bank account linking. No login required. All data stays on your device with iCloud sync.

FEATURES
- Track unlimited subscriptions and free trials
- Multi-reminder stacking (7d + 3d + 1d before charge)
- Monthly and yearly spending overview
- Category organization and tags
- Home screen widgets
- Dark mode
- iCloud sync across iPhone, iPad, Mac
- 500+ pre-loaded subscription library
- CSV export

FREE TO START
Track up to 5 subscriptions free. Upgrade to Drip Pro for unlimited tracking, trial reminders, widgets, and analytics.

Stop wasting money. Start with Drip.
```

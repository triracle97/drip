## 1. Market Overview

The subscription tracking market splits into two camps:

| Camp | Description | Examples |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Privacy-first / Manual** | No bank links, local data, lightweight | Bobby, SubTracky, SubMeter |
| **Bank-linked / Auto-detect** | Connects to financial accounts, auto-finds subscriptions | Rocket Money, Copilot, Monarch Money |

**Key market insight**: The average American holds 8.2 active subscriptions costing $219/month but estimates only $86 — a 2.5x perception gap. This is the core value proposition for every app in this space.

## 5. Competitor Weakness Summary (Exploitable Gaps)

### Gap 1: Free Trial Tracking Is Almost Non-Existent

**Problem**: 50% of users forget to cancel free trials. Only Rocket Money (bank-linked) and SubMeter (0 reviews, new) address this.
**Opportunity**: Make trial tracking the #1 headline feature. Own the keyword `trial reminder` and `free trial tracker`.

### Gap 2: Bobby Has No Android

**Problem**: Bobby is the market leader but iOS-only. Android users have no equivalent polished app.
**Opportunity**: Cross-platform (iOS + Android) from day 1 captures Bobby's overflow demand.

### Gap 3: Aggressive / Confusing Monetization

**Problem**: SubTracky criticized for $0.99/week IAP disguised as free trial. Multiple IAP tiers confuse users.
**Opportunity**: Simple, transparent pricing. One lifetime option. No tricks.

### Gap 4: No Multiple Reminders Per Subscription

**Problem**: Most apps allow only 1 reminder. Users request multiple (e.g., 7 days + 1 day before).
**Opportunity**: Offer customizable multi-reminder stacking per subscription.

### Gap 5: No Import from Apple Subscriptions

**Problem**: Users must manually add every subscription. Nobody imports from Apple's built-in subscription settings.
**Opportunity**: Deep link to Apple Subscriptions settings + guidance on adding found subscriptions; or offer screenshot-based import.

### Gap 6: Family / Shared Subscriptions Underserved

**Problem**: Only Touchbits offers Family Sharing. Couples sharing Netflix/Spotify have no good tool.
**Opportunity**: Shared subscription tracking with split cost calculation.

### Gap 7: Weak Analytics

**Problem**: Most apps show a simple monthly/yearly total. No trend analysis, no "you added 3 new subscriptions this year" insights.
**Opportunity**: Subscription creep detection — alert when monthly total increases month-over-month.
